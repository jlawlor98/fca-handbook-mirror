			<li class="subpara2" id="D461828">(aa) <p><a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a>a communication to an <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1973.html" class="autodeftext">eligible counterparty</a> that is in relation to:<a href="/instrument/2018/FCA_2018_25.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2018/25 - 01/10/2018" class="changed-by">8</a></p><ol>

			    <li class="subpara3" id="D461915">(ii) <p><a href="/handbook/glossary/G3492i.html" class="autodeftext">insurance distribution</a>;<a href="/instrument/2018/FCA_2018_25.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2018/25 - 01/10/2018" class="changed-by">8</a></p></li>
			  </ol></li>

			<li class="subpara2" id="D192"><a name="D303"></a>(c) <p>a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G421.html" class="autodeftext">financial promotion</a> approved by the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G430.html" class="autodeftext">firm</a>.</p></li>

		  </ol></div><div class="note"><p>[<strong>Note: </strong> ,<a href="/instrument/2011/2011_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2011/39 - 01/07/2011" class="changed-by">3</a>article 24(3) and article 30(1) of <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1975.html" class="autodeftext">MiFID</a>, article 17(2) of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G3482i.html" class="autodeftext">IDD</a><a href="/instrument/2018/FCA_2018_25.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2018/25 - 01/10/2018" class="changed-by">8</a>  and article 77 of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1199.html" class="autodeftext">UCITS Directive</a>]<a href="/instrument/2011/2011_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2011/39 - 01/07/2011" class="changed-by">3</a></p><a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a></div></div></div></section><section><div id="D24" class="level guidance"><div class="details"><span class="extended" id="D24">COBS 4.2.2</span><a name="D304"></a><span class="section-type guidance" title="Guidance">G</span><time><span>01/10/2018</span></time><span class="section-type"><a href="/handbook?related-provisions-for-provision=COBS 4.2.2" title="Related Provisions" class="related-provisions autodeftext">RP</a></span></div><div class="section-content"><div class="guidance"><ol>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D274">(1) <p><a href="/instrument/2007/2007_58.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2007/58 - 01/11/2007" class="changed-by">1</a>The <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2386.html" class="autodeftext">fair, clear and not misleading rule</a> applies in a way that is appropriate and proportionate taking into account the means of communication,<a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a> the information the communication is intended to convey and the nature of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G156.html" class="autodeftext">client</a> and of its business, if any<a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a>. So a communication addressed to a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1979.html" class="autodeftext">professional client</a> or an <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1973.html" class="autodeftext">eligible counterparty</a><a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a> may not need to include the same information, or be presented in the same way, as a communication addressed to a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1980.html" class="autodeftext">retail client</a>.</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D275">(2) <p><span class="xref"><span class="xrefin" id="SRC39"><a href="/handbook/COBS/4/2.html#D302">COBS 4.2.1R(2)(b)</a></span></span><a href="/instrument/2007/2007_58.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2007/58 - 01/11/2007" class="changed-by">1</a> does not limit the application of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2386.html" class="autodeftext">fair, clear and not misleading rule</a> under <span class="xref"><span class="xrefin" id="SRC40"><a href="/handbook/COBS/4/2.html#D301">COBS 4.2.1R (2) (a)</a></span></span>.  So, for example, a communication in relation to <a href="/handbook/glossary/G283.html" class="autodeftext">designated investment business</a> that is both a communication to a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1979.html" class="autodeftext">professional client</a> and a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G421.html" class="autodeftext">financial promotion</a>, will still be subject to the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2386.html" class="autodeftext">fair, clear and not misleading rule</a>.</p></li>
		  </ol><p><a href="/instrument/2017/FCA_2017_39.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2017/39 - 03/01/2018" class="changed-by">6</a>[<strong>Note: </strong> article 30(1) of <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1975.html" class="autodeftext">MiFID</a> and recital 65 to the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G3560m.html" class="autodeftext">MiFID Org Regulation</a>, article 17(2) of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G3482i.html" class="autodeftext">IDD</a><a href="/instrument/2018/FCA_2018_25.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2018/25 - 		    <li class="subpara1" id="D95"><a name="D307"></a>(1) <p>for a product or service that places a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G156.html" class="autodeftext">client's</a> capital at risk makes this clear;</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D96"><a name="D308"></a>(2) <p>that quotes a yield figure gives a balanced impression of both the short and long term prospects for the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G588.html" class="autodeftext">investment</a>;</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D97"><a name="D309"></a>(3) <p>that promotes an <a href="/handbook/glossary/G588.html" class="autodeftext">investment</a> or service whose charging structure is complex, or in relation to which the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G430.html" class="autodeftext">firm</a> will receive more than one element of remuneration, includes the information necessary to ensure that it is fair, clear and not misleading and contains sufficient information taking into account the needs of the recipients;</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D98"><a name="D310"></a>(4) <p>that names the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2974.html" class="autodeftext">FCA</a>, <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2975.html" class="autodeftext">PRA</a> or both as its regulator and refers to matters not regulated by either the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2974.html" class="autodeftext">FCA</a>, <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2975.html" class="autodeftext">PRA</a> or both makes clear that those matters are not regulated by the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2974.html" class="autodeftext">FCA</a>, <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2975.html" class="autodeftext">PRA</a> or either;</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D99"><a name="D311"></a>(5) <p>that offers <a href="/handbook/glossary/G831.html" class="autodeftext">packaged products</a> or <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1477.html" class="autodeftext">stakeholder products</a> not produced by the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G430.html" class="autodeftext">firm</a>, gives a fair, clear and not misleading impression of the producer of the product or the manager of the underlying investments.</p></li>
		  </ol></div></div></div></section><section><div id="D27" class="level guidance"><div class="details"><span class="extended" id="D27">COBS 4.2.5</span><a name="D312"></a><span class="section-type guidance" title="Guidance">G</span><time><span>25/04/2013</span></time><span class="section-type"><a href="/handbook?related-provisions-for-provision=COBS 4.2.5" title="Related Provisions" class="related-provisions autodeftext">RP</a></span></div><div class="section-content"><div class="guidance"><p><a href="/instrument/2009/2009_52.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2009/52 - 01/11/2009" class="changed-by">2</a>A communication or a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G421.html" class="autodeftext">financial promotion</a> should not describe a feature of a product or service as âguaranteedâ, âprotectedâ or âsecureâ, or use a similar term unless:</p><a href="/instrument/2009/2009_52.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2009/52 - 01/11/2009" class="changed-by">2</a><a href="/instrument/2011/2011_53.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2011/53 - 22/03/2012" class="changed-by">4</a><ol>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D329"><a name="DES556"></a>(1) <p>that term is capable of being a fair, clear and not misleading description of it; and</p></li>
		    <li class="subpara1" id="D330"><a name="DES557"></a>(2) <p>the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G430.html" class="autodeftext">firm</a> communicates all of the information necessary, and presents that information with sufficient clarity and prominence, to make the use of that term fair, clear and not misleading.<a href="/instrument/2011/2011_53.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2011/53 - 22/03/2012" class="changed-by">4</a><a href="/instrument/2013/FCA_2013_40.pdf" target="_blank" title="FCA 2013/40 - 25/04/2013" class="changed-by">5</a></p></li>
		  </ol></div></div></div></section><section><header><h2 class="crosstitle">The reasonable steps defence to an action for damages</h2></header></section><section><div id="D276" class="level rule"><div class="details"><span class="extended" id="D276">COBS 4.2.6</span><a name="DES527"></a><span class="section-type rule" title="Rule">R</span><time><span>01/04/2013</span></time><span class="section-type"><a href="/handbook?related-provisions-for-provision=COBS 4.2.6" title="Related Provisions" class="related-provisions autodeftext">RP</a></span></div><div class="section-content"><div class="rule"><p><a href="/instrument/2007/2007_58.pdf" target="_blank" title="FSA 2007/58 - 01/11/2007" class="changed-by">1</a>If, in relation to a particular communication or <a href="/handbook/glossary/G421.html" class="autodeftext">financial promotion</a>, a <a href="/handbook/glossary/G430.html" class="autodeftext">firm</a> takes reasonable steps to ensure it complies with the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G2386.html" class="autodeftext">fair, clear and not misleading rule</a>, a contravention of that <a href="/handbook/glossary/G1036.html" class="autodeftext">rule</a> does not give rise to a right of action under section 138D of the <a href="/handbook/glossary/G10.html" class="autodeftext">Act</a>.</p></div></div></div></section></div></article><div id="glossaryPageLoading" style="display:none;" class="loading-gif-overlay"><div style="display:none;" id="glossaryPageLoadingIcon" class="loading-gif gif centre-within-parent"></div></div>
